How To Use

To add a medicine, click the "Add Medicine" button. Fill out the form and then click "Add to List."

Saving Your Medicine List:

  • To save your medicine list, click "Show List" and then click the "Save" button.
  • The saved medicine list will be stored in your browser's local storage.
  • Once saved, the list will appear based on the time of day whenever you click "Show List."

Deleting Your Medicine List:

  • If you want to delete the list, click the "Reset" button.

How the Medicine List Works:

  • Morning List: From 5 AM to 11 AM.
  • Noon List: From 12 PM to 5 PM.
  • Night List: From 6 PM to 12 AM.
